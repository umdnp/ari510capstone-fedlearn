## Submission Rules

- Maximum 5 submissions per day during development phase
- Predictions must be binary (0 or 1)
- Submission format: CSV with columns: patientunitstayid, prediction
- Each submission must contain predictions for all 1,008 test samples

## Code of Conduct

- No manual labeling or external data sources
- Use only the provided training data
- Share insights and collaborate in the forum
- Be respectful to other participants

## Prizes

This is an educational competition with no monetary prizes. Recognition will be given to top performers.
